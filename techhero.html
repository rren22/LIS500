About Marques

Marques Keith Brownlee is an American YouTuber best known for his in-depth technology reviews. With over 19 million subscribers across all of his channels, he has built a reputation as one of the most influential voices in tech. Marques is also a former senior vice president of Google and has won multiple awards.

Marques' Impact

Marques has created a platform where technology meets culture. He stands out not just for his expertise but also for breaking barriers as a Black content creator. He has earned a reputation as an independent voice in the tech space, providing honest feedback and resisting corporate influence.
