About Marques

Marques Keith Brownlee is an American YouTuber who is best known for his videos reviewing technology devices. He has over 19 million subscribers across all of his channels. He is a former senior vice president of Google and has won multiple awards.

Marques Impact

Marques has built a platform where tech meets culture. He stands out not just for his expertise but for breaking barriers as a Black content creator.He has built a reputation for being independent voice in the tech space, providing honese feedback and not being swayed by corporate interest.
