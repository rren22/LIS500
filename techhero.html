Marques Brownlee

YouTuber

About Marques

Marques Keith Brownlee is an American YouTuber best known for his in-depth technology reviews. With over 19 million subscribers across all of his channels, he has built a reputation as one of the most influential voices in tech. Marques is also a former senior vice president of Google and has won multiple awards.

Marques' Impact

Marques has created a platform where technology meets culture. He stands out not just for his expertise but also for breaking barriers as a Black content creator. He has earned a reputation as an independent voice in the tech space, providing honest feedback and resisting corporate influence.


Colburns Sanders

Software Engineer

About Colburn

Colburn Sanders is a software engineer and a veteran who has navigated a unique path into the tech industry as a person of color. Originally a film student, he discovered his passion for programming through an elective course, which ultimately led him to transition into software engineering. His background in logistics from his time in the military provided him with problem-solving skills that he now applies to building technology solutions.


Colburns Impact

As a Black professional in the tech space, Sanders represents a growing but still underrepresented demographic in an industry that continues to grapple with diversity and inclusion challenges. His journey—from military service to tech, and from film studies to software engineering—highlights the multiple pathways into the industry, as well as the resilience and adaptability required to succeed. By sharing his experience, Sanders contributes to broader conversations about accessibility in tech, inspiring others from non-traditional backgrounds to pursue careers in software development.
